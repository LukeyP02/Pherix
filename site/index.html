Wrap your agent's tools and let it run. Reversible actions are journalled and undoable; irreversible ones are gated by policy. See, stop, undo — without a human watching every call.

how it arrives

Interception

Python & TypeScript SDKs, an MCP gateway, a sandbox for off-the-shelf agents.

what's allowed

Policy

Caps, allow/deny, the human gate, live-state checks — a clean slate you write rules against.

undo, reversible

Adapters

Snapshot & restore per resource. The backend does the undo — Postgres, files, more.

undo, irreversible

Compensators

Vetted semantic inverses for what can't be snapshot — charge → refund, and growing.

Get started

An open-source library — install it, wrap your tools, keep your loop and model. The hosted control plane (fleet policy, audit search, governed memory) is the layer above.
